This project started from my fascination for household textiles. I have a soft spot for ‘ household textiles ‘ like tablecloths, towels, rags, sponges, .... from here I started to combine them, then taking pictures of the combinations and started thinking why I love hese objects so much. And I came to the fact that this is because of the material, graphic patterns and the colors that are used. At first I dind’t know so well how to continue. but then I started do combine the patterns then go patterns, then connecting the used rags and started sewingen them together so it became a new household textile; tablecloth. Because who would like to eat on there used rags and mops. Then I wanted to reproduce it into a new clean tablecloth but still confront the person who would be eating on it with the idea of eating from a known mop and rag brand . The patterns in the finished product I’ve silkscreened.
