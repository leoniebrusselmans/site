This installation came out of a research of matter. Initially I just was collecting all kinds of materials. Then I searched a way to make these different materials communicate with each other and with the textile. So I’m packing, wrapping, connecting,... them. Then I wanted to let the created objects communicute on there turn with each other, and so It became a changeable installation to put them in a constant communication with each other. Furthermore, I rechearched the coincidences within combining wall paint and bale cotton, later on I I tried to put them in contrast with some ‘rougher’ materials. See, page 'furthermore'.
